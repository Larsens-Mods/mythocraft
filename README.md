# Mythocraft

## Short Description
Mythocraft brings objects and creatures from (greek) mythology into the game for you to explore, collect, use and battle with.

![Naturally generated greek temple structure with a nemean lion](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/gcOdblwO/images/0bbd49aefe7d921eac626d8e4ae46159de5c9fc9.png)

## Requirements
This mod requires [LMCommonCore](https://modrinth.com/mod/lmcc), the Fabric version additionally requires [FabricAPI](https://modrinth.com/mod/fabric-api).

## Current Features
<details>
<summary>Structures</summary>

- <b>Greek Temple</b>, generating in (Snowy-)Plains and Desert biomes, spawns the Nemean Lion and provides loot chests with mod items
</details>

<details>
<summary>Entities</summary>

- <b>Satyr</b>, a friendly creature found in wooded biomes
- <b>Nemean Lion</b>, a lion with very resistant pelt, which can drop once killed, spawns around greek temples
</details>

<details>
<summary>Armor & Weapons</summary>

- <b>Nemean Coat</b>, made from nemean lion pelts and a string, provides good protection against attacks
- <b>Helm of Hades</b>, the helm of the god of the underworld, can make the wearer invisible (monsters ignore you, unless you start attacking them)
</details>

<details>
<summary>Items & Resources</summary>

- <b>Ambrosia</b>, food of the gods, fully fills your hunger and saturation and provides 10 seconds of regeneration when eaten
- <b>Pelt of the Nemean Lion</b>, may drop when killing a nemean lion, used to craft the Nemean Coat
</details>
